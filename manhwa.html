Manhwa Titles

This page covers some of the most popular and acclaimed manhwa titles available today. From action-packed fantasy epics to psychological thrillers and reincarnation stories, these titles represent the best that manhwa has to offer. Each entry includes a brief overview with a link to the full dedicated page for more details on story, characters, and creators.

  1. Solo Leveling

    The manhwa that took the world by storm.

    • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Adventure
    • Author: Chugong (story), Jang Sung-rak / Dubu (art)
    • Status: Completed
    • Published: 2018‐2021

    Solo Leveling follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter in a world where humans fight monsters in deadly dungeons. After a near-death experience, he becomes the only person in the world with the ability to level up, setting him on a path to becoming the most powerful hunter alive. Renowned for its stunning artwork and satisfying power progression, it is widely considered the gateway manhwa for new readers and a benchmark title for the entire industry.

  2. The Beginning After the End

    A king reborn with a second chance.

    • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Isekai
    • Author: TurtleMe (story), Fuyuki23 (art)
    • Status: Ongoing
    • Published: 2018 ‐ Present

    King Grey, the most powerful monarch in his world, is reincarnated as Arthur Leywin in a new world filled with magic and monsters. Armed with memories and combat experience from his past life, Arthur navigates childhood, magic academies, and a growing darkness threatening his new world. The series stands out for its deep world-building, emotional character development, and a story that goes far beyond a typical power fantasy.

    Read more about The Beginning After the End →

  3. Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

    What if you were the only one who knew how the story ends?

    • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Apocalypse
    • Author: Sing Shong (story), Sleepy-C (art)
    • Status: Ongoing
    • Published: 2020 ‐ Present

    Kim Dokja is the sole reader of an obscure web novel, and when the world suddenly transforms into the apocalyptic scenario described in that novel, he alone knows how the story unfolds. Celebrated for its clever meta-narrative, rich lore, and deeply complex characters, it is widely regarded as one of the greatest manhwa ever written.

    Read more about Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint →

  4. The Ultimate Shut-In

    The ultimate defense is never leaving home.

    • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Comedy
    • Author: Araman (story), Maeng Joo-gyeong (adaptation), Bihyeon (art)
    • Status: Ongoing
    • Published: 2024 ‐ Present

    When his city becomes infested with monsters, Jaehyeon discovers an ability that makes him the absolute master of his apartment building — but he cannot leave it. Trapped in his high-rise apartment while the world outside burns, he must arm his neighbors and lead them beyond the building to survive the post-apocalyptic chaos. What makes it unique is that the protagonist fights monsters and expands his domain entirely from within the confines of his home, turning his biggest limitation into his greatest strength.

    Read more about The Ultimate Shut-In →

  5. Star Embracing Swordmaster

    Even the faintest star can shine if it truly wants to.

    • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Regression
    • Author: Q10 (story), Juno (art)
    • Status: Ongoing
    • Published: September 2023 ‐ Present

    When streetwise orphan Vlad finds the brothel he works for decimated by a disgraced knight, he turns to the path of the blade, pledging himself to the pursuit of power by sword. Blessed with the ability to seek wisdom from his sentient sword, Vlad embarks upon a journey of growth and peril. After being struck by black lightning, he begins hearing a mysterious voice, and when a knight cloaked in blue moonlight appears one day, his back-alley life is turned completely upside down. A story about an underdog proving that even the faintest star can shine if it truly wants to.

    Read more about Star Embracing Swordmaster →

  6. Revenge of the Iron-Blooded Sword Hound

    A loyal warrior betrayed, reborn to strike back.

    • Genre: Action, Dark Fantasy, Revenge
    • Author: Regobal Basso (story)
    • Status: Ongoing
    • Published: 2023 ‐ Present

    Vikir, a loyal warrior who devoted his life to serving a noble family, is betrayed and executed by the very people he protected. Reincarnated as his younger self, he embarks on a cold and calculated path of revenge using his knowledge of the future to dismantle his enemies one by one. One of the darker and more mature titles on this list.
