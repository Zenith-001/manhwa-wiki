  1. Chugong

    The mind behind the manhwa that changed everything.

    • Nationality:South Korean
    • Known For:Solo Leveling
    • Active Since:2014

    Chugong is the pen name of the author behind Solo Leveling, one of the most widely read and influential manhwa titles in history. He originally wrote the story as a web novel on the Korean platform Kakaopage in 2016 under the title I Alone Level Up, which quickly gained a massive following. The web novel was later adapted into manhwa format by artist Dubu, whose stunning artwork helped bring Chugong's world to life in a way that captured readers worldwide. Solo Leveling's anime adaptation in 2024 introduced his story to an entirely new global audience. Chugong is widely credited with helping define the modern dungeon hunter genre in Korean web fiction.

  2. TurtleMe

    A storyteller who built an entire world from scratch.

    • Nationality:
    • Known For:The Beginning After the End
    • Active Since:2018

    TurtleMe is the pen name of the author of The Beginning After the End, one of the longest running and most beloved ongoing manhwa today. Unlike most manhwa authors, TurtleMe originally self-published the story as an English web novel on Tapas before it was adapted into manhwa format, making it one of the few major manhwa titles to originate outside of Korea. The story's rich world-building, emotionally complex characters, and satisfying progression system have earned it one of the most dedicated fanbases in the webtoon community. TurtleMe remains actively involved in the story's development and has built a strong direct connection with his readership over the years.

  3. Sing Shong

    The architect of one of manhwa's most complex narratives.

    • Nationality:South Korean
    • Known For:Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint
    • Active Since:2018

    Sing Shong is the pen name of the writing duo behind Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint, widely regarded as one of the greatest manhwa ever written. The story originated as a web novel on Munpia before being adapted into manhwa format, and its intricate meta-narrative and deeply layered lore set it apart from virtually everything else in the genre. Sing Shong's writing is celebrated for its emotional depth, cleverly constructed foreshadowing, and the way it rewards readers who pay close attention to even the smallest details. Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint has earned a massive and passionate international following and is frequently recommended as a must-read for any manhwa fan.

  4. Soul Pung

    A writer who turned repetition into art.

    • Nationality:South Korean
    • Known For:Eternally Regressing Knight
    • Active Since:2023

    Soul Pung is the author of Eternally Regressing Knight, a standout title in the regression genre that has earned widespread praise for its grounded and realistic approach to combat and character growth. Rather than relying on flashy power fantasies, Soul Pung crafted a story where the protagonist earns every improvement through sheer persistence and willpower across countless time loops. The series is particularly admired for its portrayal of the psychological toll that endless repetition takes on the human mind, giving it an emotional weight that most action manhwa lack. Eternally Regressing Knight has built a devoted readership who appreciate its slower, more deliberate pacing and honest depiction of what it truly means to grow stronger.

  5. BK_Moon

    The creative force behind manhwa's most unique isekai.

    • Nationality:South Korean
    • Known For:The Greatest Estate Developer
    • Active Since:2021

    BK_Moon is the author of The Greatest Estate Developer, one of the most refreshing and creatively distinct isekai manhwa in recent years. Rather than following the typical power fantasy formula, BK_Moon crafted a story where the protagonist uses practical knowledge of civil engineering and architecture to navigate a fantasy world, a premise that stands out immediately in a crowded genre. The series blends humor, clever problem solving, and satisfying world-building in a way that feels genuinely original. BK_Moon's ability to make concepts like urban planning and construction feel exciting and entertaining is a testament to their skill as a storyteller.
