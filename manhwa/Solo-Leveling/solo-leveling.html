Solo Leveling

The manhwa that took the world by storm.

Quick Info

  • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Adventure
  • Author: Chugong
  • Artist: Jang Sung-rak (Dubu) — Redice Studio
  • Status: Completed
  • Published: March 4, 2018 – December 29, 2021
  • Chapters: 179
  • Platform: KakaoPage, Tapas, Tappytoon, Yen Press

Synopsis

In a world where hunters — human warriors who possess supernatural abilities — battle deadly monsters to protect mankind from certain annihilation, a notoriously weak hunter named Sung Jin-woo finds himself in a seemingly endless struggle for survival. One day, after narrowly surviving an overwhelmingly powerful double dungeon that nearly wipes out his entire party, a mysterious program called The System chooses him as its sole player and gives him the unique ability to level up in strength — something no other hunter is able to do, as a hunter's abilities are fixed once they awaken. From that moment, Jin-woo sets out on a journey from the absolute bottom to become the most powerful being in existence.

Why You Should Read It

Solo Leveling is widely considered the gateway manhwa for new readers and a benchmark title for the entire industry. Its power progression is among the most satisfying ever written, and the artwork by the late Dubu is breathtaking from start to finish. The story never overstays its welcome — it is fast-paced, clean, and endlessly entertaining from the first chapter to the last. It is one of those rare titles that delivers on every expectation it sets.

Major Themes

  • Rising from weakness to absolute strength
  • Sacrifice and the cost of power
  • Loyalty, family and protecting the ones you love
  • The burden of being chosen
  • Humanity's will to survive against impossible odds

Characters

  1. Sung Jin Woo ‐ Main Protagonist

    A courageous, kindhearted and intelligent person, Jin-woo constantly put himself in danger going on dungeon raids to put his sister in school and pay for his mother's medical bills. After being chosen by the System he transforms from the weakest hunter alive into the Shadow Monarch, the most powerful being in existence.

  2. Cha Hae-In ‐ S-Rank Hunter, Love Interest

    An S-Rank hunter and Sung Jinwoo's teammate who plays a vital role in supporting him and other hunters. She is one of Korea's finest hunters and one of the few people who can sense Jin-woo's true power before the rest of the world catches on.

  3. Go Gun-Hee ‐ Chairman of the Korean Hunters Association

    A veteran hunter and one of Korea's most respected figures who recognizes Jin-woo's potential early on. He serves as a mentor figure and plays a crucial role in the story's larger conflict between the Rulers and Monarchs.
