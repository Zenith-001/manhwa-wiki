The Beginning After the End

A king reborn with a second chance.

Quick Info

  • Genre: Action, Fantasy, Isekai
  • Author: TurtleMe
  • Artist: Fuyuki23
  • Status: Ongoing
  • Published: July 6, 2018 ‐ Ongoing
  • Chapters:225+
  • Platform: Tapas, Yen Press (print)

Synopsis

King Grey is a remarkable ruler with a prosperous reign, and yet opulence and success cannot bury emptiness and solitude. His time as king comes to an abrupt end, and he reincarnates as Arthur Leywin — a baby born in a world of magic and monsters. Equipped with memories of his previous life and the desire to protect the newfound warmth surrounding him, Art begins his journey of becoming a strong warrior once again.As the years pass, a war brews between Dicathen and the Vritra, a clan of banished deities now ruling over a faraway continent. Arthur must rise as a leader, despite his fear of becoming the war-hardened monster he once was in his past life.

Why You Should Read It

The Beginning After the End stands apart from typical isekai stories because it deeply invests in its characters and world rather than just power progression. Arthur's journey is as much about finding love, family and purpose as it is about becoming stronger. The series has been translated into over ten languages and has captivated readers worldwide, blending elements of western and eastern literature into a unique reading experience. It is one of the few manhwa to originate as an English web novel before receiving a full webtoon adaptation.

Major Themes

  • Second chances and living life with purpose
  • The true meaning of family and belonging
  • The cost of power and leadership
  • War and its impact on the innocent
  • Identity — who you were vs who you choose to become

Characters

  1. Arthur Leywin ‐ Main Protagonist

    The reincarnation of King Grey, Arthur navigates his new life determined to live differently from his past — to be loved, and surrounded by the friends and family he was void of before.A quadra-elemental mage who awakened at the age of three, his potential is unlike anything the world of Dicathen has ever seen.

  2. Tessia Eralith ‐ Love Interest, Elven Princess

    The princess of the Elenoir Elven Kingdom and one of Arthur's closest companions. She is a powerful mage in her own right and plays a central role in both the political and emotional core of the story.

  3. Sylvie ‐ Arthur's Draconic Bond

    Arthur's draconic companion who represents his first meaningful connection beyond his immediate family and provides a direct link to the Asuran world. Her emotional support proves as valuable as her combat assistance throughout Arthur's journey.

  4. Agrona Vritra ‐ Main Antagonist

    Leader of the Vritra clan and Alacrya, Agrona initiated the devastating war against Dicathen that dominates much of the narrative. Every major conflict Arthur faces can be traced back to Agrona's schemes, making him the architect of Arthur's greatest challenges.

  5. Reynolds & Alice Leywin ‐ Arthur's Parents

    A family of former adventurers who settled down after their stint in the famed adventuring party the Twin Horns. Good, caring and supportive parents who play a pivotal role in how Arthur's new life turns out.

  6. Eleanor Leywin ‐ Arthur's Younger Sister

    Commonly known as Ellie, she is the younger sister of Arthur and daughter of Alice and Reynolds. Her character grows significantly as war engulfs Dicathen, forcing even the youngest generation into active roles.

Adaptations

An anime television series adaptation produced by Studio A-Cat aired from April 2 to June 18, 2025 on Fuji TV's +Ultra programming block. Crunchyroll streamed the series with an English dub produced concurrently. A second season is set to premiere in April 2026.
